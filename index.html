Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know ahead of scheduling your medical visit.

Do I need a prior referral to visit a specialist? +

No, our clinic accepts direct self-referrals for all departments including Cardiology and Dental Wellness. However, some external health insurance plans may mandate a primary care referral for payment coverage.

How quickly are diagnostic laboratory results returned? +

Standard blood panels, pathology, and diagnostic imaging services are typically processed with fully comprehensive digital reports available within 24 to 48 hours.

What should I bring to my initial medical consultation? +

Please remember to bring a valid photographic government identity card, your active health insurance document, and a comprehensive written list of any active prescriptions or past historical medical reports.
